About Me

Hi! I am Cameron Walsh. Welcome to my page!

I was born and raised in Plymouth, MA. From there I left to attend Michigan State University. After graduating with a chemical engineering degree in 2012, I moved to Lafayette, LA to work for a large oilfied services company. After almost 6 years of service, I left the company so I could move to sunny Wauchula, FL and better take care of my family.

Currently, I'm back in school learning web development in UCF's Coding Bootcamp and will be looking for employment after. In the meantime, I spend my time exploring Florida and playing fetch with my 4 dogs. None of them have yet to give the stick back yet.
